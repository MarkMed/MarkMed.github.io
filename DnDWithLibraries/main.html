InteractJS

Drag and Drop library with very smooth animations. Accept touch drags. High compatibility with browsers. Includes Resizing, Snaping, and Multi-Touch features, and also includes Tap, Double-Tap and Long-Tap events.

Shopify Draggable

Drag and Drop library developed by Shopify with a big bundle of DnD fueatures. Unique Dorpzone, Lists, CSS display modes, Collision, and Snappable.
