Welcome to New Babbage

New Babbage is the largest city on planet microTech and a major trading hub, serving as headquarters for software and high-end electronics manufacturer microTech. The landing zone is also a haven for small tech companies. Large public spaces are enclosed under domes to protect the inhabitants against the freezing elements of the planet

Featured Places

Interstellar Spaceport

New Babbage Interstellar Spaceport is the main spaceport serving New Babbage on microTech. Built on the side of a mountain, it overlooks a frozen lake and the city of New Babbage beyond.

Promenade

Promenade is the entertainment area of the domes, where people hang out to have drinks (or consume substances) at Wally's Bar. Eddie Parr is located in this area. There are also several shops located in this area:

  • Lily Monte Salon
  • Aparelli
  • Whammer's
  • Thaw
  • Oza Neo-Imperial Cuisine

The Plaza

The Plaza is located on the larger part of the "main dome" of The Dome Structure, this area is the corporate business park of New Babbage. This section houses several different shops and offices
